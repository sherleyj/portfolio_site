Coop
Coop is a 2-6 player game of deception and trickery. Play with your friends and take actions allowed by your drawn character cards - or don't. But if you get called out prepare to pay the consequences!

This game utilizes React on frontend and Express for it's API. Since this is a quick ~15 minute game it uses Redis for its in memory database. Hosted on AWS, uses Nginx as a reverse proxy, and PM2 as a process manager.
